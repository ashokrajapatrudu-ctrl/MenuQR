RESTAURANT STORY

Our Story

Since 2016, Amigos has grown from a familiar campus stop into a shared IIT Kharagpur memory. It is where project nights softened into dinner, birthdays found a table, alumni returned for one more favourite plate, and everyday meals became part of hostel conversations.

Ten years later, Amigos remains a place built around warmth, generous food and the people who made it feel like home.
